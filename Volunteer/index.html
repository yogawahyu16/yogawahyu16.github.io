BACKGROUND



Waste management in Indonesia is an actual problem accompanied by population growth which can impact increasing the amount of waste generated. In some research was conducted to show important priority aspects that must be prioritized in waste processing. Aspects that need to be considered in handling and consider the selected waste management strategy in order of priority with



using the AHP (Analytical Hierarchy Process) method, including policy government, environment, financing, health, and public perception Mahyudin (2010:99). According to Mulasari, 2012, waste is an object or material that is no longer used by humans to be discarded. The societal stigma regarding waste is disgusting, dirty, and others so that they must be burned or disposed of properly. View junk data on the average landfill on June 4, 2021, on the SIPSN website (Information System National Waste Processing) there are 36,975,616.66 tons per year. Caused by a lack of public awareness in the management and use of waste. So that it happens accumulation of garbage and environmental pollution, the role of young people is a factor most important in creating a better future. Indonesia is a country 2nd largest waste producer in the world after China in 2019 "quoted from the site" binus.ac.id".



Building public awareness is not as easy as turning the palm of the hand. There is a need for cooperation from all parties, both the government community or third-parties as supporters. There is a need for a direct socialization movement about waste management that can encourage community participation in waste management (Rizal, 2011). Self Service Indonesia is a volunteer social movement in Indonesia that focuses on environmental education, especially the younger generation to create awareness of the importance of a healthy environment for all living things. In volunteer self-service activities, carry out work programs that will invite the community, especially the younger generation to contribute to creating a healthy and clean environment.
